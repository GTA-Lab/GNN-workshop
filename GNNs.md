# Graph Neural Networks Workshop (Part II)

## Part II: Graph Neural Networks in PyG

In [the second part](https://gta-lab.github.io/graph-neural-networks), you'll dive deep into the world of graph-structured data and learn how GNNs can be leveraged to solve a variety of tasks, including node classification, graph classification, and graph regression. You'll have the opportunity to implement GNN models using the PyTorch Geometric library and apply them to real-world datasets, such as citation networks and molecular structures. Go to [gta-lab.github.io/graph-neural-networks](https://gta-lab.github.io/graph-neural-networks) for more information.
